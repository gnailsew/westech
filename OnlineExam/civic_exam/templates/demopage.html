Data Analyst in E-Commerce

E-commerce Industry Overview

  • What is E-Commerce?

    Business model enabling a firm or individual to conduct business over an electronic network, typically the internet.

  • Types of E-Commerce:

    • Consumer-to-Consumer (C2C): Ebay/Amazon, Airbnb, Uber/Didi, T-mall/Taobao,
    • Business-to-Consumer (B2C): Amazon, Alibaba/JD, Freelance
    • Business-to-Business (B2B): Square, Amazon AWS, MS Azure
    • Consumer-to-Business (C2B): Google Adsense

  • Product: physical/digital/service and etc.

BA/DA in E-Commerce

  • Business Analyst

    • Under CFO or COO
    • Business Intelligence (BI) team
    • Support various department: warehouse/UX design/marketing/operation/intl.

  • Data Analyst

    • Under CTO
    • Data Analytics/Data Science team
    • Support marketing/stragey/BI and work with technology closely

Both can serve as internal consultant roles.

Important Metrics & KPIs

  • Sales/Transaction: GsSales (FP/MD), GM%, RetRate, NetSales, NetGM%, InvST ...

  • Traffic: Visits, Conversion Rate (repeat/new visitor)

  • Customers: New/Active Customers, Retention, NPS

  • Marketing: CPA (CPC/PLA), Est.Marketing Spend (Paid Search/Retargeting/Affiliate/Social Media/CSE/SEO/Email etc.)

Data Analytics Full Cycle

Oversee every process in typical E-Commerce to look out for potential issues, problems and potential for optimization to boost business performance.

General steps:

  1. Setup solution for request
  2. Data mining: collection/cleaning/massage
  3. Analytical analysis: A/B testing, regression modeling, seasonality/trending, etc.
  4. Summarize findings and insights: provide solution and/or recommendation

Hiring in E-Commerce

  • Data Analyst: STEM major preferred

  • Business Analyst: MBA/B-school preferred or equivalent working experience

  • Common Interview Question:

    • SQL, Excel (8+ out of 10)
    • Scripting language like Python/Java (DA 8+/BA 6+)
    • Business questions related: metrics, logic, decision/strategy
    • Communication and problem solving skills
